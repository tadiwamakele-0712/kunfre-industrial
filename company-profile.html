About Us

Kunfre Hardware (Pvt) Ltd, trading as Kunfre Enterprise, is a registered Zimbabwean company and trusted supplier of industrial-grade sealing, fastening, electrical, instrumentation, and security products. Over 5+ years we have built a reputation as a dependable partner to mining, oil & gas, construction, agriculture, water utilities, power generation, and manufacturing sectors across Zimbabwe.

On-Site Technical Support

Our engineers visit client sites for plant inspections, product selection, borehole surveys, instrumentation commissioning, and security system installations across Zimbabwe.
