About Us

Kunfre Enterprise

Kunfre Hardware (Pvt) Ltd, trading as Kunfre Enterprise, is a registered Zimbabwean company and trusted supplier of industrial-grade sealing, fastening, electrical, instrumentation, and security products.

Over 5+ years of operation, we have built a reputation as a dependable partner to the mining, oil & gas, construction, agriculture, water utilities, power generation, and general manufacturing sectors across Zimbabwe. Our engineers and product specialists work closely with clients to select the right solution for the most demanding environments.

Legal name: Kunfre Hardware (Pvt) Ltd
Trading as: Kunfre Enterprise
Address: 32065 Mabvazuva, Ruwa, Harare, Zimbabwe
Motto: Pinnacle of Reliability

Our Mission

To supply precision-grade industrial products and expert field services that keep Zimbabwe's critical infrastructure, agriculture, mining, and manufacturing sectors running reliably.

Our Vision

To be Zimbabwe's most trusted industrial supplier — recognised for quality, technical expertise, and dependable delivery across sealing, fastening, flow control, water solutions, instrumentation, and security.
